Methodology — exact filters, selection rule, weighting formulas, rebalance rules
① Eligibility filters · all variants
  1. Not an ETF — exclude all index/commodity/bond ETFs
  2. Price ≥ ₹50 — exclude penny stocks
  3. Price ≤ ₹1,00,000 — your requested upper bound (functionally no cap; no NSE equity above ~₹30K)
  4. 90-day avg turnover ≥ ₹25 Cr — liquid enough to enter/exit without major impact
  5. Sharpe (1y rolling, rf=0) ≥ 2.0 — quality threshold
  6. Δ5d (Combined JADE, 5 trading days) > 0 — short-term momentum is positive
② Selection rule

Among all eligible stocks, compute the composite score: score = Δ5d × Sharpe then take the top 5 by score (descending).

③ Weighting formulas

All three variants pick the same 5 stocks. They differ only in how capital is split among those 5. For each variant, weight stock i by: wi = Sharpeip ÷ Σ Sharpejp where p = 2 (Conservative · 🟢), 5 (Aggressive · 🟡), or 8 (Max · 🔴).

④ Rebalance rules
  • Cadence: monthly
  • Rebalance day: first trading day of each calendar month
  • Transaction cost: 25 bps per side (modelled as 12.5 bps × turnover)
  • Holding period: exactly one month (sell, buy, hold)
  • No leverage, no shorting, no derivatives
⑤ Backtest stats shown

CAGR and Max DD on each card are the average across four windows: 1y, 2y, 3y, and 4y all ending on the latest available date. All return calculations are point-in-time — selection uses only data dated ≤ rebalance date.
